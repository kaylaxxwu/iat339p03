About Me

I am a research-driven UX designer with curiosity. Whether it's graphic design or product design, I always question why a design is made in a particular way. Because I like the UX design approach, which involves problem-solving through research and user-centered design, I moved from the graphic design profession to UX. I employ UX research strategies like survey and co-creation workshop to identify user problems and needs, and then design the experience to fulfill their needs. When creating user interface design, I combine my graphic design skills with tools like Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, and Figma to create visually appealing designs with the right visual hierarchy.
